About

We are an Irish owned and operated environmental consultancy and testing facility. Easca Environment's name is derived from the Irish word for easy, easca, and we strive to make your environmental testing as easy as possible.

We are passionate about applying science to deliver integrated scientific and environmental services and solutions to society. Our company is home to highly qualified and experienced scientists who deliver a wide range of analytical and specialist consulting services to our clients in the agricultural, environmental, food, and pharmaceutical sectors. We pride ourselves in the quality of our work and the confidence you can place in our results.

Easca Environmental operate to the highest standards of quality so that customers can have the confidence to act on our results. We take great pride in providing a friendly and prompt service every time you need us. Our personal guarantee is based on the confidence we place on our Team's ability to resolve your specific requirements within an agreed timeframe.

What makes us different?

By adopting progressive digitization of our services, we offer the quickest most efficient means of getting you tests results and tracking your land nutrient levels.

  • Select and order the most appropriate testing for your needs from our comprehensive test services.
  • The sample kit will we dispatched to you on the same day as you ordered.
  • Each sample test ordered will be assigned a unique 'Sample Reference' so you can track the processing of the sample.
  • By logging into 'Your Portal' you will see the status of your samples.
  • Once you have received your sample kit, you can 'Submit Details' of the sample through 'Your Portal'.
  • After submitting the sample details online, you can return the sample to us in provided packaging.
  • You will be informed via email when your results are available for viewing.
  • You can view the sample results and details report through 'Your Portal'.
